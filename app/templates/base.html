What is this web for ?

This web serves as an application for a CBR system. The purpose of this work is to recommend cocktails according to a set of ingredients.

Where can I find the cocktails ?

There is a catalog with more than 100 cocktail recipes in the "Cocktails" tab.

What happens if the cocktail that I want is not in the catalog?

Not a problem, in "Recommender" you can choose the ingredients that you want for your cocktail and we will provide for you a recipe.

How many ingredients can I choose?

You can choose as many as you want with a minimum of 2 ingredients.

After receiving a personalized recipe I noticed that I am allergic to some ingredients, what can I do?

Just email us at cocktailmasters@mixologist.com and we will provide you with a new recipe according to your needs.

Can I trust the recommended recipe ?

Most of the time we offer feasable recipes, however it may happen that the recipe does not follow what you are looking for. For that reason we ask you for your opinion. That way you can help us continue improving!
